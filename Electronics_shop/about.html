Who are we?

ConnectedGadgets is the trading name for the specialist retail division of incredible connections PTY Ltd in South Africa. All our staff are trained regularly by the manufacturers in house to make sure we can help you as much as possible to choose the right printer, smart Phone or laptop for your home or business use. Training covers every aspect of print solutions from how the printer works including paper choice, colour hues and speed right up to 'cost-per-click' print management same goes for phones and laptops, just ask for assistance and you will be assisted by a relevant person according to what you are purchasing .
