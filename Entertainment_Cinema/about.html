About us

Regal Cinema 16 Pty Ltd is a company with limited liability incorporated in accordance with the company laws of the Republic of South Africa.

To most South Africans, the word “movies” is synonymous with the name “Regal Cinema 16”; the biggest movie exhibitor by far (more than double the size of its nearest competitor), Regal Cinema 16 owns some 60-65% of the market, boasting some 55 movie complexes in South Africa (more than 400 screens and 64 000 seats) with 154 state-of-the-art 3D cinemas, giving Regal Cinema 16 the largest 3D footprint in this territory, as well as 6 complexes in neighbouring territories.
